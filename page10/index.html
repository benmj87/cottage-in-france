ARRIVE AT THE HOUSE and park on the grassy verge. Climb the steps to the front door and enter. In front of you is the charming, beamed living room with an ornate, cast iron, wood burning stove. From here turn left into the ground floor bedroom or go straight ahead into the dining room with it's door to the courtyard. In the courtyard there is a table and chairs and a brick-built barbecue.

From the dining room is the door and stairs leading to the mezzanine bedroom overlooking the sitting room.

The kitchen is straight ahead and is fitted with a modern gas hob and oven. There is a washing machine, microwave, toaster etc.

In the left hand corner of the dining room is the door to the shower and toilet.
