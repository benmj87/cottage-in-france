THE VILLAGE is perfect for those who enjoy walking, trips to the beach and French rural life with plenty of quiet roads and tracks for cycling or walking along the river or in the nearby woods. Neighbouring villages offer good eating, weekly markets and places of historical interest. It is an ideal base for exploring the region, visiting war memorials and sites, local markets for fresh produce, enjoying fine food and wine and generally just taking it easy.
